What's included

Everything you need to be
audit-ready

SimpleAML covers the full compliance workflow for accounting practices under Tranche 2 AML/CTF legislation.

Client Risk Profiling
Complete CDD for every client. Capture entity type, jurisdiction, designated service, beneficial owners, and risk rating in one form.
Evidence Vault
Secure document register for DVS identity checks, PEP screening, sanctions checks, and all CDD evidence — audit-ready at all times.
Firm Risk Assessment
Document your firm-level ML/TF/PF risk assessment covering all designated services, client types, and geographic exposure.
Policy & Approval
Upload your AML/CTF program, track Part A and Part B checkpoints, and record senior manager approval with a full audit trail.
Personnel Due Diligence
Track initial and ongoing PDD for all AML/CTF-related staff. Record identity checks, background screening, and fit & proper assessments.
Training Register
Log AML/CTF training completions for all staff. Track expiry dates and get alerts when training is due for renewal.
