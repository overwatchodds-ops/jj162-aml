01

Scope of service

SimpleAML, provided by Click Seed Pty Ltd (ABN 87 656 256 567), is a compliance assistance tool designed to record customer due diligence (CDD) and other AML/CTF checks. It is intended to support firms in managing their AML/CTF obligations under Australian law.

SimpleAML is a compliance register. It records that checks and assessments have occurred, and points to where underlying evidence is stored. It is not a document management system, vault, or archive.

Important: If you are unsure about your AML/CTF obligations, seek independent legal advice from a qualified Australian lawyer with expertise in financial crime compliance.

03

Evidence and document management

SimpleAML is a compliance register only. It:

  • Records that checks and assessments have occurred;
  • Points to the location of underlying evidence; and
  • Does not store, hold, encrypt, or manage original compliance documents, identity verification records, or other artefacts.

The collection, maintenance, secure storage, and protection of all underlying evidence — including identification documents, screening records, and compliance program documents — is the sole responsibility of the user and their firm.

Your obligations as the user: You are responsible for securely storing all underlying evidence, maintaining records for the mandatory 7-year retention period required under the AML/CTF Act, encrypting and protecting sensitive client documents, and ensuring continuity of access to records in the event of staff changes or system changes.

Click Seed Pty Ltd, SimpleAML, and any associated parties accept no responsibility for the loss, theft, unauthorised access, or destruction of documents and artefacts held by the user.
