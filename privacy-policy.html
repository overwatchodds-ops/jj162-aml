01

Who we are

SimpleAML is a free AML/CTF compliance tool developed and operated by Click Seed Pty Ltd (ABN 87 656 256 567), a company registered in New South Wales, Australia.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the SimpleAML website (simpleaml.com.au) and the SimpleAML web application. By using our site or app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you have any questions, you can contact us at any time using our Contact Form.

02

What information we collect

We collect a limited amount of information depending on how you interact with SimpleAML.

Information you give us directly:

  • Your email address if you contact us via email
  • Any information you voluntarily include in correspondence with us

Information collected automatically:

  • Pages visited on simpleaml.com.au and approximate time of visit
  • Your general location (country or city level, not precise location)
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • Anonymous usage patterns collected via Google Analytics (see Section 5)

Important — app data: Compliance data you enter into the SimpleAML app (client records, risk assessments, firm details, documents) is currently stored in your browser's local storage and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. See Section 6 for full details.

Information we do not collect:

  • We do not currently require account registration or login
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not collect precise geolocation data
  • We do not collect biometric data
03

How we use your information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries you send us by email
  • To understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it (via analytics)
  • To monitor the performance and availability of the SimpleAML application
  • To comply with our legal obligations under Australian law

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We do not use your information for advertising profiling or targeted marketing.

04

Firebase & Google services

SimpleAML uses Firebase, a platform developed by Google, as part of our technical infrastructure. Firebase is used for website hosting (Firebase Hosting) to serve the SimpleAML site and application reliably.

As a result, your connection to SimpleAML passes through Google's infrastructure. Google may process certain technical data (such as IP addresses and request logs) as part of providing this hosting service. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Future change — user accounts: We intend to introduce optional user registration and login functionality in a future version of SimpleAML, using Firebase Authentication. When this feature is introduced, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify users clearly. At that point, account information (such as email address) will be stored on Firebase's servers, which are located outside Australia. We will provide full details of this before the feature goes live.

Click Seed Pty Ltd and SimpleAML are the data controller for any personal data processed in connection with our services. Google acts as a data processor in relation to Firebase services.

05

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the SimpleAML website. Google Analytics collects anonymous information about your visit, including which pages you view, how long you spend on the site, and how you arrived.

This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. We use it solely to improve the website and understand what content is most useful to visitors.

Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to collect this information. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time by:

  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Using your browser's privacy or incognito mode
  • Adjusting your browser's cookie settings to block third-party cookies

For more information on how Google uses data collected via analytics, visit policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

06

Your compliance data in the app

The SimpleAML application is designed with your data privacy and security in mind. All compliance data you enter into the app — including client records, risk assessments, firm details, training records, and uploaded documents — is stored in your browser's local storage on your own device.

This means your compliance data is not transmitted to, or stored on, any server operated by Click Seed Pty Ltd or any third party. It exists only on the device and browser you use to access the app.

As a consequence of this architecture, you are solely responsible for:

  • Maintaining regular backups of your compliance data using the app's export function
  • Ensuring your data is retained for the 7-year period required under AML/CTF legislation
  • Securing the device and browser on which your data is stored
  • Any loss of data resulting from clearing browser storage, changing browsers, or changing devices

We strongly recommend exporting your data regularly and storing a backup in a secure location.

Note: If you clear your browser's local storage or use the app in a private/incognito window, your data will not persist between sessions. Always use the export function to maintain your records.

07

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles, you have rights in relation to your personal information, including the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Make a complaint if you believe we have mishandled your information

Because we collect very limited personal information (primarily via email enquiries), most of these rights are straightforward to exercise. Simply contact us at clickseedaustralia@gmail.com and we will respond promptly.

If you are not satisfied with our response to a privacy complaint, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

08

Children

SimpleAML is a professional compliance tool intended for use by adults operating in regulated industries. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

09

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, particularly as we introduce new features such as user accounts. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of SimpleAML after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

If we introduce features that materially change how we handle your personal data — such as the introduction of user accounts and Firebase Authentication — we will make this clearly visible on the site before those features go live.
