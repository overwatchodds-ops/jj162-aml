The Requirement

What is the AUSTRAC Compliance Report?

The Annual Compliance Report is a mandatory legal requirement under Section 47 of the AML/CTF Act. Every year, reporting entities must submit a report via AUSTRAC Online confirming how they have met their obligations.

For accounting practices, the first mandatory reporting period begins on 1 July 2026. You must lodge your first report between 1 July and 30 September 2027 (covering the 2026-27 financial year). Failure to submit within this window can result in significant civil penalties.

SimpleAML is your Lodge-Ready Summary. You cannot "upload" a document to AUSTRAC; you must manually answer their online questionnaire. SimpleAML tracks your activity all year so that when the 2027 window opens, you have every answer ready to copy-paste into the portal in minutes.

<
What It Covers

What does the report need to address?

To be audit-ready, your internal registers must align with AUSTRAC's reporting framework. SimpleAML captures the data required for these 8 critical areas:

  • Governance & Oversight — Practice details and formal appointment of your AML/CTF Compliance Officer (AMLCO).
  • ML/TF/PF Risk Assessment — Now includes Proliferation Financing (PF) risk, inherent ratings, and your firm’s risk appetite.
  • AML/CTF Program — Evidence of senior management approval and your 3-year independent evaluation schedule.
  • Employee Due Diligence — Vetting history for staff in "key positions" and annual fit-and-proper declarations.
  • Training Register — Dated records of AML/CTF training for all relevant staff to meet your annual training obligation.
  • Customer Due Diligence (CDD) — A summary of verified clients, including beneficial owner and PEP/Sanction screening results.
  • Transaction Monitoring — Evidence of ongoing monitoring and the identification of any "Designated Services" provided.
  • Reporting Activity — A log of Suspicious Matter Reports (SMRs) or a "Nil" record if no reportable matters occurred.
How SimpleAML Works

A Simple Compliance Summary

SimpleAML isn't a complex enterprise platform. It is a compliance register designed to keep your records in one place. The Compliance Report is a simple PDF export that summarises the data you’ve entered—giving you a clear 'snapshot' of your firm’s status at any moment.

What the report actually provides:

  • One-Click PDF Export — Converts your digital registers into a clean, readable document.
  • Data at a Glance — Summarises your firm details, risk ratings, and training logs so you don't have to hunt through different tabs.
  • The 'Cheat Sheet' for AUSTRAC — While you still have to lodge manually on the AUSTRAC website, this report gives you the numbers and dates you need to type in.
  • Audit Trail — Every time you generate the report, it logs the date. This proves to an auditor that you've been checking your compliance regularly.
  • Privacy First — The report only contains the text data you've entered. We don't store your clients' sensitive ID documents or files.

Think of it as your 'Workpaper'. Just like you prepare a year-end file to support a tax return, this report supports your AUSTRAC filing. It records that you did the work—the actual evidence stays in your firm's existing filing system.
