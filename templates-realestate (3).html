How to use these templates

Four steps to a complete program.

Complete the risk assessment first, then work through Part A and Part B. Once done, record your approval in SimpleAML.

1
Download the templates

Download all three Word documents. They open in Microsoft Word, Google Docs or any compatible editor.

2
Complete the risk assessment

Start with the ML/TF/PF Risk Assessment — your program must be built on this. Replace all [bracketed fields] with your agency's details.

3
Complete Part A and Part B

Fill in Part A (governance) and Part B (CDD procedures). Both must be approved by your Principal or LIC before 1 July 2026.

4
Upload to SimpleAML

Upload your completed documents to the Policy & Approval section of SimpleAML. Your program is now documented and audit-ready.
