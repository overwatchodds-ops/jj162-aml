AML identity verification tools for accountants — honest comparison

From 1 July 2026, Australian accountants must verify client identities as part of their CDD obligations. This guide explains the two types of tools you need, reviews the main providers, and helps you pick the right combination for your practice.

March 2026 12 min read  ·  Chris Wong, CPA

First: there are two different jobs to do

Before comparing tools, it’s important to understand that “AML identity verification” actually covers two distinct obligations under the AML/CTF Act — and they require different types of tools.

The two jobs

1. Identity verification (DVS): Confirming your client is who they say they are — by checking their ID documents against government databases. This is about document authenticity.

2. PEP and sanctions screening: Checking whether your client appears on lists of politically exposed persons, sanctioned individuals, or adverse media. This is about risk profile.

Both are required. A tool that only does DVS doesn’t satisfy your screening obligations. A tool that only does PEP/sanctions screening doesn’t satisfy your identity verification requirements. Most practices will need at least one tool from each category — or a single platform that covers both.

⚠

Common mistake: Some practices assume that sighting a passport or licence satisfies their AML/CTF identity verification requirements. Manual document sighting is legally acceptable, but electronic verification via a DVS-connected service provides a stronger, more defensible audit trail — and is faster for you and your client.

VerifiMe also includes PEP/sanctions screening, biometric checks, and a full AML/CTF compliance portal. Pricing starts around $50/month plus per-verification fees.

StackGo IdentityCheck
DVS · KYC · CRM-Embedded · PEP/Sanctions (add-on)
CRM-embedded

StackGo IdentityCheck embeds KYC and AML verification directly inside your CRM — HubSpot, Salesforce, or Xero. Rather than opening a separate portal, you trigger a verification from the client’s contact record, and the results write back automatically. A privacy layer keeps sensitive PII out of your CRM and behind MFA-authenticated admin access. Pricing is usage-based (pay per check), with PEP/sanctions screening available as an add-on from around USD$0.75 per check.

Strengths
  • No new software — runs inside your CRM

  • Usage-based — no monthly minimum
  • 200+ countries, 10,000+ document types
  • Audit trail native to your CRM
Limitations
  • Only useful if you run HubSpot, Salesforce or Xero
  • PEP/sanctions screening is an add-on, not included
  • Initial configuration required
  • Pricing in USD
Pricing
Pay-per-check — no monthly minimum. Identity verification checks are usage-based (see stackgo.io/identitycheck for current rates). Our recommendation by practice type

There is no single right answer — the best combination depends on your practice size, existing software, and client profile. Here’s our recommendation by situation:

Sole practitioner, no CRM
NameScan + VerifiMe
Use NameScan for fast, pay-per-search PEP/sanctions screening. Use VerifiMe for DVS identity verification. Between them you have full CDD coverage at low cost with no CRM needed.
BGL Simple Fund 360 user
BGLiD + NameScan
BGLiD is already in your subscription — use it first for DVS verification of SMSF clients. Add NameScan for any screening needs not covered by BGLiD’s built-in checks.
Practice on HubSpot or Salesforce
StackGo IdentityCheck
If your client records already live in HubSpot or Salesforce, StackGo is the cleanest option — verification happens inside your existing tool with automatic write-back. Add the PEP/sanctions add-on for complete coverage.
High-value or complex clients
VerifiMe
For practices with HNWI clients, complex trusts, SMSFs or foreign nationals, VerifiMe’s full-service platform and reusable wallet provides the strongest audit trail and the smoothest client experience.
Data sovereignty is a priority
OneClickVerify

Just need PEP/sanctions screening
NameScan
If you’re already handling DVS through another method and just need fast, pay-per-search PEP and sanctions screening, NameScan is the simplest and most cost-effective option.

Our recommendation for sole practitioners and small firms

If you are a sole practitioner or small accounting firm — under 10 staff, no CRM, not on BGL — the honest answer is simpler than the market makes it look.

Recommended combination

NameScan for PEP and sanctions screening — VerifiMe for DVS identity verification. Between them, you have complete CDD coverage: identity verified against government databases, and clients screened against every major watchlist. No CRM required. No complex setup.

Why NameScan for screening

Pay-per-search with no monthly minimum means you only spend money when you actually onboard a new client. A small firm doing 50 new clients a year spends around 0 total on a scan pack — less than a tank of fuel. There is no subscription ticking over in quiet months, no annual contract, and no minimum commitment beyond the initial pack of 50 scans.

Why VerifiMe for identity verification

The reusable identity wallet is a genuine advantage for small practices. When you ask a new client to verify through VerifiMe, they create a wallet once. If they later engage a lawyer, conveyancer or financial adviser who also uses VerifiMe, they don’t need to verify again — they simply share their existing credentials. That is a real quality-of-service benefit you can explain to clients at onboarding.

The ~0/month subscription is the main cost to weigh. If you are bringing on fewer than five new clients a month, the per-client cost is relatively high. But for a small practice that values a clean, auditable, end-to-end verification process — and doesn’t want to stitch together multiple tools — VerifiMe is the most complete standalone option available.

Already on BGL? Adjust the combination

If your practice uses BGL Simple Fund 360 or Simple Invest 360 for SMSF administration, BGLiD is already included in your subscription at no extra cost. Use BGLiD for SMSF clients and NameScan for everything else. You may not need VerifiMe at all — BGLiD covers DVS verification and AML/CTF screening for your SMSF client base, and NameScan handles any clients outside BGL.
